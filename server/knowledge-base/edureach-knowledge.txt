INSTITUTION OVERVIEW
EduReach University is a premier educational institution established in 2005, dedicated to providing world-class education in engineering, management, and business studies. Located in the heart of Hyderabad, Telangana, India, EduReach has grown to become one of the most sought-after universities in South India. Our commitment to excellence in education, research, and student development has made us a beacon of quality higher education.

The university is affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad and approved by AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education), New Delhi. We maintain an active partnership with international universities and industry leaders to ensure our curriculum remains globally relevant and industry-focused.

MISSION AND VISION
Mission: To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values to become responsible professionals and leaders who contribute positively to society and industry.
Vision: To be recognized as a center of excellence in engineering education, management studies, and research with a global perspective and local relevance.

A: Yes, merit scholarships, need-based scholarships, sports scholarships, and government scholarships for SC/ST are available.

Q: Is hostel accommodation provided?
A: Yes, mandatory for first-year students. On-campus hostel available for 1850 students plus nearby PG accommodations.

Q: What is the placement rate?
A: 92% for engineering, 95% for MBA, 88% for BBA. Average salary Rs 7.2 LPA with range from Rs 3.5 to 42 LPA.

Q: Are internships mandatory?
A: Yes, 2 mandatory internships (6-8 weeks each) for all B.Tech students during 5th and 6th semesters.

Q: Do you have international student admissions?
A: Yes, admissions open for international students from SAARC and non-SAARC countries. Visa and hostel support provided.

Q: Is there after-hours support available?
A: Yes, 24/7 emergency support for medical, safety, and hostel-related issues. Regular office hours Monday-Friday 9am to 5pm.

Q: Can I defer my admission?

Q: How many clubs and activities are available?

Q: What career support is provided?
